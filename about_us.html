Grand Fortunes Group Introduction

Grand Fortunes Group is a comprehensive industrial conglomerate integrating services in logistics trade supply chain, industrial park development and operation, and food supply chain services. With a strong foundation and deep resources in the Greater Bay Area, the Group enjoys a high reputation and goodwill in the industry.

The Group's main business segments include Cross-border logistics, industrial park development and operation, and food supply chain.

The Group's core values are: "Integrity, Pragmatism, Focus, Innovation, Responsibility".

Founder Introduction

Founder's Message

Only through relentless forging can we astonish the world

Mr. Jin Haiming serves as the Chairman of the Grand Fortunes Group, President of Guangdong Grand Fortunes Supply Chain Science and Technology Co., Ltd., President of the Guangzhou Customs Declaration Association, a specially invited member of the Guangdong Provincial Association for Science and Technology from Hong Kong and Macau, Founder of Wei Shipei Cargo (Guangzhou) Co., Ltd., and the first President of the Nansha Foreign Trade Quality Service Providers Promotion Association (preparation stage); he has also served as a People's Congress representative in Guangzhou's Yuexiu District and the first President of the Guangzhou Huadu Airport Logistics Association.

Entrepreneurial Experience

In 1995, he founded Wei Shipei Cargo (now renamed Guangdong Xinruihengdao Investment Holding Group Co., Ltd.), focusing on international logistics.

In 2001, he established Guangzhou Grand Fortunes Investment Co., Ltd., focusing on the development and operation of high-tech digital industrial parks.

In 2021, he founded Guangdong Grand Fortunes Supply Chain Science and Technology Co., Ltd., focusing on the food supply chain.

Business Philosophy

Since its inception, Mr. Jin Haiming has implemented and adhered to the core business philosophy of "Integrity as the Foundation, Innovation as the Soul, and Development as the Mainstay". He wholeheartedly promotes the spirit of extreme professionalism to meet the multi-level needs of customers, committed to creating new value for customers and partners. He strives to build faster, better, and more competitive advantages, winning high trust and support from customers, partners, and all sectors of society.

To better empower customers with innovation, Mr. Jin meticulously builds a learning organization, promoting a top-down development concept of learning above all. Learning ability, thinking ability, innovation ability, and action ability have become key assessment standards for enterprise talents, laying a solid knowledge foundation for the enterprise to keep pace with the times and lead the logistics supply chain industry. Using the concept of development to solve problems encountered in growth and helping customers achieve rapid development are Mr. Jin's and the enterprise's unwavering pursuit.

Social Responsibility

Mr. Jin actively participates in public welfare, enthusiastically donating to the Guangya Education Development Fund, serving as the Vice Chairman of the Guangya Education Development Foundation, and supporting the various charitable activities of the Guangdong Times Public Welfare Foundation.

During the first half of the 2022 pandemic, he contributed ideas to promote dual circulation and ensure the smooth operation of the automotive industry supply chain.
