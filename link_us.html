Food Supply Chain

The Group's subsidiary, Grand Fortunes Supply Chain Science and Technology Co., Ltd., will focus on developing the food supply chain business, investing in the Nansha Free Trade Zone at the core of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area to build a new platform for the future aggregation ecology of global premium food.

Based on Nansha's superior business environment and high-quality government services, it will take advantage of the efficient and low-cost customs clearance of Nansha International Deepwater Port and Nansha Comprehensive Bonded Port Area, and build a high-standard, low-carbon and intelligent management digital industrial complex for the supply chain of high-quality foodstuffs by building a digital industrial complex for the supply chain of high-quality foods in Nansha, which integrates the research and development, processing, auctioning, warehousing, distribution, collection and distribution of foodstuffs, bonded, testing and quarantine, trading, exhibition and live broadcasting, and will become the digital trading center for the supply chain of high-quality foods, the transfer base for the food processing industry of Macao's Portuguese-speaking system, the base for supplying Hong Kong, Macao and Southeast Asia of high-quality foods of Guangdong Province, and the base for live broadcasting and auctioning and displaying international foodstuffs, and the base for international food collection and distribution.
