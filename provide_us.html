Tianhe E-PARK Project Introduction

The "Guangzhou Tianhe E-park project", invested, developed, and operated by Grand Fortunes Investment Co., Ltd., a subsidiary of the Group, has a total construction area of 273,000 square meters and is a "national technology innovation industry base" tech industry park, serving as a national-level technology enterprise incubator, with over 400 high-tech companies settled in the park. Among them, intelligent innovation enterprises account for 85%, and the park's companies have an annual total output value of over 10 billion and tax revenue exceeding 500 million.

Located in Tianhe Smart City, at the intersection of the Guangzhou-Shenzhen-Hong Kong and Guangzhou-Zhuhai-Macao technology innovation corridors, it is a key node of the Guangzhou technology innovation axis and the most dynamic central science and innovation district (CTD), forming a "one district, two cores" development pattern together with the Tianhe Central Business District (CBD).

The project covers a total area of 75,000 square meters, with a total construction area of 273,000 square meters, aiming to create an "international smart innovation industry base", integrating Class A industrial R&D office space, low-density garden office space, comfortable rest spaces, and business and commercial support facilities.

The park's five major functional features: intelligent innovation, industrial R&D, enterprise incubation, industrial aggregation, supporting services.
