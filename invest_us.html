Cross-border Logistics

Guangdong Xinrui Hengdao Investment Holding Group Co., Ltd., founded in 1995, has nearly 30 years of experience in import and export trade. It is a leading provider of comprehensive solutions focusing on cross-border logistics, logistics finance, supply chain logistics, foreign trade comprehensive services, and logistics industrial park infrastructure construction. It has created several professional logistics service brands around cross-border business.

Among them, the annual number of import and export customs declarations exceeds 8 million; taxes paid on behalf of clients exceed 15 billion; more than 2400 logistics cargo planes served; the total volume of import and export trade exceeds 250 billion; the air freight import and export throughput exceeds 500,000 tons.

VIE is a cross-border air transport business brand under the group, leveraging years of market accumulation and resource accumulation in the import and export logistics industry, deeply engaging in big data and artificial intelligence technology, gradually developing into an innovative enterprise providing a full chain of air cargo cross-border logistics + foreign trade comprehensive services. It covers a business network of 72 countries and regions, with an annual air cargo business volume exceeding 60,000 tons.

CTD, a professional air transport comprehensive ground logistics service brand under the group, established in 2004, has developed a mature and complete airport ground comprehensive service system over more than 20 years, becoming the largest third-party ground service provider at Guangzhou Baiyun Airport. It serves more than 5400 cargo charter flights annually; handles over 300,000 tons of air cargo; has a professional service team of over 300 people; owns over 5,000 square meters of cargo operation space; and is designated as a long-term cooperation service provider by more than 30 air cargo companies.
